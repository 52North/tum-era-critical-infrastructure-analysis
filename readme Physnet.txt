Linear Load Flow Calculation for the Power Transmission System of Valparaiso Region for the Infrastructure Risk Analysis in Chile Using Pypsa


Juan Felipe Chaves Santander
Supervisor: Hugo Fernando Rosero, MSc

München,  Agust 2020

This code generates the electrical transmission network of Valparaiso region in Chile with the information provided in the files C1_EPN_ExposureNodes.geojson, C1_EPN_ExposureLines.geojson and the information of the online wbsite of Electrical National Coordinator, INFOTECNICA. Once the network was implemented, a DC load flow with PyPSA was performed. The result of this analysis is the electrical power through the transmission lines. To validate the resuls, the values are compared with measurements available online. All the information and results of buses and lines are stored in geojson and shape files.
